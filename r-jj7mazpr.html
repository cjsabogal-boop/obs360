The Triad of E-Commerce

Choosing a platform is not just about fees; it's a trade-off between Traffic (Marketplaces) and Control (DTC). Amazon and Walmart offer built-in audiences but demand conformity. Shopify offers total brand freedom but requires you to build the audience yourself. Explore the radar chart below to visualize these fundamental trade-offs.

Ecosystem Analysis

Each platform offers a unique ecosystem of tools. Amazon and Walmart focus on logistics and advertising within their walls. Shopify focuses on an open app store allowing infinite customization.

Future Trajectories

Amazon

Focusing on global expansion and advertising dominance. Becoming "Pay to Play". Increased focus on video/social commerce (Inspire feed) to combat TikTok.

Walmart

Bridging the Omnichannel Gap. Leveraging 4,700 stores for fulfillment/pickup. Actively recruiting high-quality 3rd party sellers to expand SKU count.

Shopify

Moving Up-Market (Enterprise/B2B) and solving the "Traffic Problem" via Shop App and deep integrations with YouTube/Instagram/TikTok.

Is there a "Correct Balance"?
