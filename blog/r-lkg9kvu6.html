Amazon Warehousing & Distribution (AWD)

AWD is Amazon's low-cost bulk storage solution designed to hold your inventory before it moves to fulfillment centers. It helps you save on storage fees, avoid FBA stock limits, and automate replenishment. Explore the tools below to see if AWD is right for your business.

Cost-Effective

Up to 80% lower storage rates than FBA during peak season, with no seasonal surcharges.

Automated Replenishment

Amazon's algorithms automatically send inventory to FBA to prevent stockouts.

Flexible Storage

Replenishments from AWD don't count against FBA restock limits, giving you more capacity.

Multi-Channel

Use one pool of inventory to fulfill orders on Amazon and other sales channels.

Amazon Global Logistics (AGL)

AGL is Amazon's service for shipping inventory from international manufacturing hubs directly into its network. It simplifies freight forwarding, customs, and tracking. Understand your critical responsibilities as an importer before you begin.

End-to-End Service

Manage the entire journey from factory pickup to Amazon facility delivery.

Flexible Freight

Choose from Standard or Fast Ocean (FCL/LCL) and Air Freight options.

Customs Support

Amazon-arranged customs brokers help facilitate the clearance process.

Integrated Tracking

Monitor your shipment's journey in real-time directly within Seller Central.

Critical: Importer of Record (IOR) Checklist

Amazon will NOT act as the Importer of Record. This responsibility is 100% yours. Use this checklist to understand your key legal and financial obligations when importing goods.

AGL Onboarding Process

1

2

Set Payment

Choose your payment method (USD via Seller Central or CNY with business license).

3

Set Up IOR

Complete the IOR profile, sign the POA, and purchase a customs bond if needed (~5 business days).

4

Book Shipment

Once your profile is approved, book your first freight shipment via the "Send/Replenish" workflow.
